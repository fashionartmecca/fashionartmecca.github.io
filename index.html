Fashion Art Mecca is a five day exhibition from April 20 to April 24 showcasing underground Nashville designers, artists, and brands. Think Art Basel meets Fashion Week done Nashville DIY style. FAM 2016 will feature a variety of events including gallery crawls, capsule shows, pop-up shops, and warehouse parties.

BUY EARLYBIRD TICKETS!!!

4/20: TATTOO POP UP

Kickoff party at Grimey's from 4-8pm! Pop up tattoos designed by Ann Catherine Carter. The first 20 will be able to choose from a flash and get the tattoo of their choice will a certified artist. Chill and meet other FAM guests and hear live music!

4/21: STREETWEAR POP UP / NASHVILLE RAP

Starting in the late afternoon, designers will set up individual pop-up shops with merchandise for sale. The pop-up market will flow into a night of performances from everyone's favorite Nashville rappers. (Location TBA)

4/22: DREAMHOUSE GALLERY/FRATERNITY AS VANITY RELEASE/AFTER PARTY

The day will begin at the Dream House (2505 Blair Blvd.) with cockatails and installations by a number of local artists. The night will continue with a good old fashioned house party after the gallery show, which will also be a party for the record label Fraternity as Vanity as they release their new compilation UNITY IN DIFFERENCE: MONISTIC PLEASURES. (Address will be announced closer to event!)

4/23: RTW DESIGNER SHOWCASE / WAREHOUSE PARTY

RTW Designers and artists will showcase their unique collections in a gallery-style presentation at Elliott Hall at Tennessee State University. Followed by a nighttime warehouse dnace party at Meal Ticket (1030 3rd Ave S.)

4/24: ISABEL SK PRESENTATION / AFTERPARTY

The final day will feature a presentation of the brand new full collection from FAM 2016 co-creator Isabel SK at the American Legion (3204 Gallatin Pk.) This will flow into a dance party with 90s to current hip hop and hype dance music.
